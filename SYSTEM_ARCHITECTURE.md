
# How the Nexus AI Team Works

## 1. The Team
*   **Working Together:** Each AI helper has a special job and works with others to reach a goal.
*   **Smart Planning:** The AI looks at past successes before deciding what to do next.

## 2. Main Features
*   **Checking Work:** A "Fact Checker" looks for mistakes. If it's not sure, it tries again.
*   **Doing Actions:** The AI can talk to other computer systems like servers or web tools.
*   **Transparency:** You can see every thought and action the AI makes in the "Thought Stream."

## 3. Where It Helps
*   **Storms:** Helping cities stay safe during bad weather.
*   **Computer Errors:** Finding and fixing website errors automatically.
*   **Shopping:** Helping stores buy the right items at the right time.

## 4. Technical Details
*   **AI Brain:** Uses Gemini-3-Pro-Preview for smart thinking.
*   **Design:** A clean and modern look that shows the AI talking in real-time.
